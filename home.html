Hello World!

Hello there, you have reached the site of Harry's. Please feel free to explore and enjoy yourself in the lovely background music ^^

I have just graduated from the University of Toronto this June! During these years of studying, Harry has acquired a handful of useful skills in computer science. He made several side-projects in his interests. These include a Chrome extension that analyses the comments in the YouTube video, a visualizer for Conway's Game of Life, a widget that helped him book badminton courts (at the time he was obsessed with it), several web-based games, and this site itself. Harry loves using the knowledge he just acquired into practice to accomplish something intriguing and profound. Therefore, his recent work is an ESP32 based bike computer. How exciting that is!

In his daily life, Harry manages different roles. He is an excellent team player, a responsible developer, a self-taught graphic designer, a bass singer, and a photographer. Upon the day he got his first camera and computer, Harry began his journey to the digit world. He has hitherto been shooting with DSL and using Photoshop for more than seven years and has let the skills to brew with the time elapsed. You can find his works by navigating through the sidebar to the right. (Caution: Mobile data usage)
